Value Added Program

Power BI Workshop

Data Visualization & Business Intelligence

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science conducted a Value Added Program on Microsoft Power BI, equipping students with hands-on skills in data visualization, dashboard creation, and business intelligence reporting.

Students learned to connect diverse data sources, transform raw data into meaningful insights, and design interactive dashboards — bridging the gap between data science theory and real-world analytics practice. The program provided practical exposure to one of the most in-demand tools in the industry.

The initiative underscores the department's commitment to enhancing employability by offering students skill-building opportunities beyond the regular curriculum.

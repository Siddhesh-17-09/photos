Industrial Visit — Learning Beyond the Classroom

[ Insert Photo of Students at Jio Reliance Corporate Park Here ]

To bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry exposure, students of the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science participated in industrial visits to Jio Reliance Corporate Park, Ghansoli, Navi Mumbai.

On 5th February 2026, Third-Year students visited the campus as part of an educational industrial tour. During the visit, students gained valuable insights into emerging technologies, large-scale data storage systems, and modern industry practices. They learned how data is managed, processed, and secured in real-world applications, along with exposure to cutting-edge technological developments shaping the digital ecosystem. The visit proved to be an informative and enriching experience for all participants.

On 6th February 2026, Second-Year students also visited Jio Reliance Corporate Park, Ghansoli, Navi Mumbai for an industrial visit aimed at enhancing their practical understanding of industry operations and technological infrastructure. The visit provided exposure to professional environments and contemporary technological practices.

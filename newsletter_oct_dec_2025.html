1 October 2025

Dussehra Celebration

Embracing Culture & Knowledge

The Department celebrated Dussehra with great joy, devotion, and enthusiasm, marking the triumph of good over evil and the pursuit of knowledge.

The celebration commenced with a sacred Saraswati Poojan, performed by the Head of Department, Dr. Shankar Patil, invoking blessings for wisdom, learning, and academic excellence. Faculty members and students actively participated in the rituals, creating a serene and spiritually uplifting atmosphere.

Traditional values blended beautifully with the department's modern technological spirit, reminding everyone of the importance of ethics, knowledge, and continuous growth.

"May knowledge always guide us towards the path of success and righteousness."

9 December 2025

Avishkar 2025

Showcasing Innovation & Team Spirit

Students enthusiastically participated in Avishkar 2025, a platform dedicated to promoting research, innovation, and creativity among young minds.

The participants demonstrated remarkable teamwork, collaboration, and dedication while presenting their ideas and projects. Their active involvement reflected not only technical competence but also a strong passion for innovation and problem-solving.

The event served as a valuable opportunity for students to explore research-oriented thinking, exchange ideas, and gain exposure to competitive academic environments.
 

21 – 29 December 2025

Uttarakhand Trip

Journey Beyond Classrooms

Following the successful completion of Semester V examinations, students of the AI&DS department embarked on an unforgettable unofficial trip to Uttarakhand.

This journey offered a refreshing escape from academic routines, allowing students to explore the scenic beauty of the mountains, experience new cultures, and strengthen friendships beyond the classroom environment.

From serene landscapes to adventurous experiences, the trip created lasting memories and reinforced the importance of balance between academic life and personal well-being.

24 – 31 December 2025

NSS Camp 2025

Sahangoti Village, Alibaug

The NSS volunteers actively participated in a week-long camp under the guidance of NSS Program Officer Prof. Videet Kambale at Sahangoti Village, Alibaug.

Students carried out multiple impactful activities including cleanliness drives, awareness campaigns, and rallies to promote social responsibility and community welfare.

One of the key highlights was the organization of a medical camp, providing essential healthcare support to the villagers. The camp instilled values of empathy, leadership, and teamwork among students.

23 September 2025

Smart India Hackathon 2025

The students of the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science showcased exceptional enthusiasm and ingenuity through their active participation in the prestigious Smart India Hackathon 2025 — a nationwide initiative aimed at solving real-world challenges through technology.

Working collaboratively in teams, participants engaged with diverse problem statements spanning domains such as smart automation, sustainability, and digital transformation. The students demonstrated strong technical expertise alongside critical thinking, creativity, and a solution-oriented mindset.

This experience bridged the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation, fostering innovation and preparing them to tackle industry-level challenges with confidence.

"Innovation thrives where curiosity meets real-world challenges."
 

25 September 2025

Deep Dive into AI

Exploring Concepts, Uses & Innovations

The Data Pioneers Club successfully conducted an enriching knowledge-sharing session. What made this event truly impactful was its peer-led approach — third-year students stepped into the role of mentors and speakers.

They delivered insightful sessions on fundamental AI concepts, practical applications across industries, and the latest technological advancements shaping the future. The event created an interactive learning environment, encouraging discussions, curiosity, and active participation.

Such initiatives reflect the department's commitment to fostering a collaborative learning culture and empowering students to become future-ready innovators.

"The future belongs to those who understand and innovate with AI today."
